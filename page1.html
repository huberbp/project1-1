About Booze Creek

What is Booze Creek Resort?

Booze Creek Resort is a winter vacation destination that caters to all ages. Located in Booze Creek, West Virginia, we have been operating since 1956 and have celebrated more than 60 years in business. Booze Creak Resort has been owned and run by the McKenzie family since it's creation in 1956. During all of our years of operation, the McKenzie family has always had the same motto, "Fun for the whole Family". Their mission, started over 60 years ago, has always stayed the same, to provide a wholesome and fun experience where families can come and spend quality time, while making quality memories.

What can I expect from Booze Creek Resort?

Visitors to the Booze Creek Resort will be treated to top-tier dining services, lodging, and of course ski and snowboard slopes. More information on each of these can be found on our website The resort opens every year in October and stays open until the end of March. Even when it's not snowing in Booze Creek, our top of the line snowblowers keep the slopes operational throughout the whole season.

What are the different packages available?

Day-Pass

  • Access to the slopes from 8 a.m. to 8 p.m.
  • Pass is only valid for a single day
  • Pass required to board lifts
  • $49 on weekdays
  • $69 on weekends
  • $25 per day for ski or snowboard rentals

Week-Pass

  • Access to the slopes from 8 a.m. to 8 p.m.
  • Pass is valid for 7 days from purchase
  • Pass required to board lifts
  • One free meal per day at the diner
  • $300 for a week
  • $20 per day for ski or snowboard rentals

Season-Pass

  • Access to the slopes from 8 a.m. to 8 p.m.
  • Pass is valid for the whole season
  • Pass required to board lifts
  • Free ski and snowboard rentals
  • 5 free days to stay at the lodge
  • $700 for the season
